## 📋 Executive Summary

We propose building a turnkey QR-powered instructional video system for your mushroom grow bag products. When a customer scans the QR code on any grow bag, they'll see a **branded, product-specific video tutorial** — no app download, no registration, instant access.

**One scan. One video. Growing success.**

---

## 🎯 The Problem

- Customers buy grow bags but don't know the optimal growing process
- Paper instructions get lost or are hard to follow
- YouTube searches return generic or conflicting advice
- Support tickets and refunds increase from failed grows
- No visibility into which products are actually being used

---

## 💡 The Solution

**QR → Landing Page → Video Tutorial**

1. Each grow bag has a unique QR code (static or per-SKU)
2. Customer scans with phone camera (no app needed)
3. Mobile-optimized page loads instantly with product-specific video
4. Video shows exact steps for that mushroom variety
5. Analytics track every scan — you know what's selling and where
